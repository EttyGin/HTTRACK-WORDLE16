Sedecordle: Solve 16 Wordles at Once

The Sedecordle follows the classic rules of the Wordle game, only you have to guess 16 target words at the same time and you have 21 guesses to solve this puzzle. Play Sedecordle with words from 4 to 6 letters, and use the daily game mode to solve the same words with your friends every day. Can you guess the 16 hidden words in twenty-one tries?

How to play the Sedecordle Game?

  1. Enter the first word

    You have 21 tries to solve 16 hidden words. To start the game and get hints, just enter any word that will be in 16 parts of the game at the same time.

  2. Find out what letters are in hidden words

    Follow the letter clues to solve all 16 words. If the letter is guessed correctly and is in the correct place, it will be highlighted in green, if the letter is in the word, but in the wrong place - in yellow, and if the letter is not in the word, it will remain gray.

  3. Try to guess all 16 words

    Guess the word in each part of the game to win the game! After completing the game, you can easily share your result on social media or save a screenshot of your puzzle.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What are the rules of the Sedecordle game?

    You have 21 guesses to solve 16 target words. Follow the color clues on the letters and keyboard to solve the puzzle.
    Color marking:
    The letter isn't in the target word at all.
    The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
    The letter is in the word and in the correct spot.

    To win the game, you need to guess all 16 words. (all letters are green).

  2. What is the difference between Sedecordle and Wordle?

    The Sedecordle follows the classic Wordle rules, only you have to solve sixteen words at the same time and you have 21 tries to do it. Since there are 16 hidden words, the color hints on the keyboard keys are divided into 16 parts according to the side of the game. This game has gained popularity because it is much more difficult and interesting than the classic version of Wordle.

  3. What is the best word to start the game?

    At the beginning of the game, try to use a word without repeating letters and with as many vowels as possible, such as the word "RADIO". However, mathematician Grant Sanderson found that the best starting word is “CRANE" or “SLOTH”, which includes frequently used letters.

  4. Can I play Sedecordle in different languages?

    You can easily change the game dictionary in the settings in the top left corner. At the moment, Sedecordle is available in American English, UK English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Russian, Polish, Ukrainian, Swedish, Irish, Czech, Greek, Turkish, Indonesian, and Filipino.

  5. How to enable daily Sedecordle mode?

    You can turn on the daily mode in the game settings and solve the same words every day with your friends. The word chain changes every 24 hours.

  6. Can I change the number of letters in hidden words?

    You can easily change the number of letters in target words. Open the game settings and select the appropriate value for the number of letters, at the moment the game is available with words from 4 to 6 letters.

What is Sedecordle?

Sedecordle is one of the many Wordle alternatives floating around the internet these days like Dordle, Quordle, Octordle, and the like. Players must guess 16 five-letter words in 21 tries or less. The game grid is designed to accommodate all of the words so you can check the impact of your input on all of them at the same time.
The color tile hint rules remain the same as in Wordle. When the correct letter is in the correct place for the corresponding word in this case, the tile turns green. If a letter belongs to a word but is in the wrong place, the tile turns yellow. The tile turns gray if the letter does not appear at all in the word.
